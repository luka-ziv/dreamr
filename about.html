Alek Zivkovic

Alek is a young and ambitious videographer and photographer from Hamilton, ON. Since 2020 Alek has created a name for himself within the creative community. Having worked alongside other creators and mentors capturing behind-the-scenes moments of Shaedon Sharpe’s NIL partnership with Porsche Louisville, C’est Bon’s Rolling Loud pop up shop, and Canadian NBA star Shai Gilgeous Alexander’s birthday party at Soluna in Toronto.

Alek has a knack for compelling storytelling from working in the nightlife industry across Toronto, to creating personal brands for individuals and businesses. He had followed Hamilton Tiger Cats defensive end, Mason Bennett, capturing moments from the season and piecing together a personal brand for the athlete.
